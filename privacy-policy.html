Privacy Policy

Card Kingdom — Last updated: July 8, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Card Kingdom ("the app", "we") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our multiplayer card game application.

Information We Collect

  • Account information: When you create an account we collect your email address and display name. If you sign in with Google or Facebook, we receive your name, email address, and profile picture from that provider.
  • Gameplay data: Game results, coin balance, virtual-currency transaction history, and matchmaking activity, stored to operate the game.
  • Purchase data: When you buy coin bundles, the purchase is processed by Google Play or the Apple App Store. We receive a purchase token to verify the transaction and credit your coins. We never see or store your payment card details.
  • Analytics and crash data: We use Firebase Analytics and Firebase Crashlytics to understand app usage and fix crashes. This includes device model, operating system version, and anonymized usage events.

How We Use Your Information

  • To operate multiplayer games, matchmaking, and the coin economy.
  • To verify in-app purchases and prevent fraud.
  • To fix bugs and improve the app.
  • To respond to support requests.

Virtual Currency

Coins in Card Kingdom are a virtual currency for entertainment only. Coins have no real-money value, cannot be redeemed, withdrawn, or exchanged for cash or anything of value outside the app.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. Data is shared only with the service providers required to run the app: Google Firebase (authentication, database, analytics, crash reporting) and Google Play / Apple for purchase processing. Other players can see your display name, avatar, and game statistics.

Data Retention and Deletion

Your data is retained while your account is active. You can request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us at the support email below. We will delete your data within 30 days of a verified request.

Children

Card Kingdom is not directed at children under 13 (or the minimum age in your jurisdiction), and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. The app includes simulated gambling mechanics using virtual currency and is intended for adult audiences.

Security

Data is transmitted over encrypted connections and stored in Google Firebase with access rules that restrict each user's data to that user. Coin balances can only be modified by our server, never directly by clients.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by the "Last updated" date above.
